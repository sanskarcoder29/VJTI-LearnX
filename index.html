Welcome to VJTI LearnX

VJTI LearnX is a one-stop study platform designed especially for students of Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI), Mumbai.

This platform was developed by Sanskar Wanjari, a passionate computer engineering student and a blind developer, with the vision of making academic resources easily accessible for every student.

The goal of VJTI LearnX is simple — students should focus on learning, innovation, and academic growth, without wasting time searching for scattered study materials across multiple platforms.

Here you will find subject-wise resources including Notes, Books, and Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs) for various engineering subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Digital Logic Design, Engineering Mechanics, Data Structures, Computer Organization, and many more.

VJTI LearnX aims to create a smarter and more organized learning ecosystem where knowledge is shared, resources are structured, and students can easily access the materials they need to succeed in their academic journey.

What You Can Find Here

  • Subject-wise Engineering Notes
  • Reference Books and Study Resources
  • Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs)
  • Well Organized Academic Materials
  • Continuously Updated Study Content

Vision

The vision of VJTI LearnX is to build a centralized learning hub for VJTI students where academic resources are easy to access, regularly updated, and structured in a way that helps students learn efficiently and focus on what truly matters — their education.
