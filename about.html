About VJTI LearnX

VJTI LearnX is a student-focused academic resource platform created to simplify access to study materials for students of Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI), Mumbai.

The goal of this platform is to provide a centralized place where students can easily find subject-wise study resources such as notes, reference books, and previous year question papers.

Instead of searching through multiple websites, students can access all essential academic materials in one organized platform.

Our Vision

The vision of VJTI LearnX is to build a collaborative learning ecosystem where knowledge is shared openly and students can focus on learning, innovation, and academic growth without worrying about finding study materials.

About the Developer

VJTI LearnX was developed by Sanskar Wanjari, a Computer Engineering student at Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI), Mumbai.

Despite being a blind developer, Sanskar is passionate about technology, programming, and building tools that make education more accessible and organized for students.

This platform reflects his vision of using technology to make academic resources easier to access for everyone.
